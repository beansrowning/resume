\section{\faSearch\ Profile}
\Profile{
  I am a Surveillance Epidemiologist and expert R programmer working with the Division of Foodborne, Waterborne,
  and Environmental Diseases. My responsibilities include running national passive
  surveillance of four enteric pathogens and serving as a founding member of the
  Informatics Unit of the National Surveillance Team. I work at the crossroads between Epidemiology, Informatics, and Data Science. \\
  \\
  In addition to my surveillance work, I am the current chair of the CDC/ATSDR R Users Group where I help to coordinate 
  monthly meetings, devise trainings, and foster collaborative learning. \\
  \\
  My interests include: Surveying spatio-temporal trends of Shiga-toxin producing \textit{Escherichia coli} and \textit{Salmonella},
  unsupervised and semi-supervised machine learning, mathematical modelling, and data visualization.}
