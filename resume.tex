\Profile{
  I am a Surveillance Epidemiologist working with the Division of Foodborne, Waterborne,
  and Environmental Diseases. My responsibles include running national passive
  surveillance of four enteric pathogens and serving as acting coordinator for the
  Informatics Unit of the National Surveillance Team. \\
  \\
  In addition to my surveillance work, I am a proficient R programmer and current chair of the CDC/ATSDR R Users Group
  where I have help coordinate with leadership to devise trainings and supply key IT resources for the agency. \\
  \\
  My interests include: surveying national trends of Shiga-toxin Producing Escherichia coli and Salmonella,
  unsupervised anomaly detection on highly dimensional surveillance data, mathematical modelling, and data visualization.}
